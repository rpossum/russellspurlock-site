About Me

I’m Russell Spurlock — a Certified Flight Instructor and Commercial Pilot in Florida. My daily work as a CFII keeps me sharp and connected to the fundamentals, while my experience in jet and turboprop operations has given me valuable real-world perspective. Together, these experiences position me as a well-rounded pilot ready to grow in professional turbine flying.
